What the game actually renders today. Screenshots captured from the live JS build (./gradlew captureScreens, headed + 1:1); sprites are the real atlas; values are transcribed from scenes/*.kt. This is the reproduce baseline — match it exactly first.

This file = current truth. The aspirational glass-panel mockup moved to refinement-mockup.html (clearly labeled NOT-current). design-tokens.json is the authoritative token contract.
Logical canvas is 1000×1500 (desktop/web) / 1000×2200 (Android). The HUD is flat text — no panels. Planes are sprites from the atlas below, not ✈ emoji.
Premise (refine-toward, not shown here): Air War 2142 is an alt-history reimagining — WWII air warfare evolved 200 years without abandoning its roots: riveted prop-planes + energy weapons + sci-fi HUDs, a steampunk-style fusion anchored to WWII. See PROMPT.md §1. These screenshots are the current state, not the theme.

REAL SCREENS (captured from the running game)

1 — Main Menu
Ocean gradient + big scrolling island discs. "AIR WAR" 56px cyan, "2142" 184px gold. Two 840×80 translucent button bars; gold focus caret at left of the focused one. Bottom hint, faint white.
2 — In-game HUD
Flat text over live ocean/islands/cloud-ellipses. SCORE (cyan) · PAUSE (white) · LOOPS (orange) top row. Player is the small blue plane sprite. FIGHTERS / FUEL · ENERGY bottom row.
3 — Paused
Full-canvas black @0.7 over frozen game. Left-aligned text: "MISSION PAUSED" 56px; "[ P ] Resume flight" green; "SETTINGS & INPUTS" white; "[ Q ] Abandon mission" faint.
4 — Settings (from game)
#1E272C bg. "FLIGHT CONTROLS" 72px cyan title. 3 steering rows (active = cyan/teal + gold caret). Orange ± steppers, green/red SFX & DEBUG toggles, green "RESUME GAME" (or red "SAVE & EXIT" from menu).

⚠ Observed engine artifact (not a design choice): glyphs with vertical strokes (T/L/I) render slightly doubled in the JS build — visible as "FLIGHTi CONTiROLiS". Logged for the team; ignore for design intent.
